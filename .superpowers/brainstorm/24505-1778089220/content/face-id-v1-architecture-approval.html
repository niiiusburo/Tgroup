V1 Behavior

Register

Register from an existing customer profile, or from a no-match search after staff selects the correct customer.

Search

High-confidence match opens the customer. Medium confidence shows top candidates. No match keeps the image for manual registration.

Improve Over Time

If a customer already has Face ID, new captures add another sample instead of replacing prior samples.

Failure Rules

No face, many faces, blurry image, or engine offline produce clear staff-facing retry states and do not block normal customer work.
