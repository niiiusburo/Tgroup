No-Match Rescue Flow

Updated from your feedback: keep the captured image and let staff attach it to a searched customer.

V1 Smooth Flow
Capture once, then match or register
1. Capture Face
Staff scans the walk-in once from Face ID search.
→
2. Recognition Result
High confidence opens customer. Low confidence shows candidates. No match keeps the captured image.
→
3. Staff Search
Staff searches by name, phone, or customer code and picks the right customer.
4. Register Captured Face
The same image is attached to the chosen customer. No second camera step unless quality failed.
→
5. Confirmed Customer
Customer profile shows Face ID registered and future Face ID searches can find them.

When staff registers a captured face to a customer who already has Face ID, what should happen?

A

Add Another Face Sample

Keep old embeddings and add the new capture. Better recognition over time, but more samples per customer.

B

Replace After Confirmation

Ask staff to confirm replacing the existing Face ID. Simpler records, but loses old angle/lighting coverage.
