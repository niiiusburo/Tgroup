# IDEA.md

> Non-authority inbox for TGClinic product ideas. Ideas are raw material until
> they are promoted into `ARCHITECTURE.md`, `BEHAVIOR.md`, `DESIGN.md`,
> `DECISIONS.md`, `product-map/`, or a runbook.

## Rules

- Capture rough ideas here when they are not yet approved implementation work.
- Mark whether the idea is raw, accepted, rejected, superseded, or promoted.
- If an idea affects schema, permissions, payments, customer records, external integrations, deployment, or migration data, add authority checks before implementation.
- Ideas do not override product-map unknowns or accepted decisions.

## Template

```markdown
## IDEA-YYYYMMDD-NN: Short Title

Status: Raw
Scope:
Problem:
Proposed shape:
Authority checks:
- `ARCHITECTURE.md`
- `BEHAVIOR.md`
- `product-map/domains/<domain>.yaml`
- `product-map/unknowns.md`
Open questions:
```
